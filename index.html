WHAT IS rECursion CLUB's MOTIVE?

rECursion is the official coding club of ECE branch in SGSITS.
We altogether work as a team which helps ECE students to get assistance and mentorship in coding. Our aim is to build a community which helps students to enhance their coding skills and build a good career in software development field.
